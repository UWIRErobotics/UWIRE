8a6: Connectionism: Decision Making

Localized networks can be used to model decision making. Recall that in making a decision, we seek to identify a plan that will satisfy as many of our goals to the greatest extent possible. This characterization allows for a fairly straightforward implementation in terms of a localized connectionist network. 

The DECO (DEliberative COherence) theory by Thagard and Millgram gives such an account. In DECO, nodes represent either goals or actions. (In addition, there is a special node, goal priority, which serves to activate the goals relevant to the decision problem at hand.) There are two kinds of links between nodes, namely facilitation and incompatibility. Facilitation links are the excitatory links and represent the connections between actions that facilitate or help to achieve certain goals. Incompatibility links are the inhibitory links and represent the connections between actions that hinder the achievement of certain goals.

Suppose you face the problem of what to do after graduation. There are a number of goals that you might seek to achieve such as learning more, enjoying life, or working towards a life of leisure by retiring early.

Note that, in the DECO framework, links are always bidirectional. That is, if node A has an excitatory link to node B, then node B has an excitatory link (of the same weight) with node A.

This example can be represented via the network above. The actions that facilitate goals (e.g., “graduate school” and “understand more”) have excitatory, facilitating links. Conversely, actions that hinder the facilitation of certain goals (e.g., “graduate school” and “corporation”) have inhibitory, incompatibility links. The network as a whole represents the constraints that apply among the options that we need to consider.

As with the cat/rat example, making the decision amounts to activating the network (via the Goal Priority node), allowing the activation to spread, and waiting until the network settles. If a unit, e.g., “graduate school”, settles with high activation, then that is an action or goal that that we have decided to follow. Units that are deactivated when the network has settled, we have decided not to follow.
